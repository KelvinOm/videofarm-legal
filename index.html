VideoFarm

A personal tool for generating and publishing short-form videos.

VideoFarm is a self-hosted application used by a single operator to generate short-form videos and publish them to their own connected social media accounts: YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok.

What it does

  • Generates short video scripts and renders 9:16 vertical videos with synced captions.
  • Connects to the operator's own social media accounts via official OAuth flows.
  • Publishes the finished videos to the operator's own accounts on the platforms listed above.

TikTok permissions requested

When connecting a TikTok account, the app requests the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to read the connected creator's basic profile info (open id, display name, avatar).
  • video.upload — to upload videos to the creator's TikTok inbox for the creator to finalize manually.
  • video.publish — to publish videos directly to the creator's own TikTok profile on their behalf, with the caption and privacy setting they configured in VideoFarm.
