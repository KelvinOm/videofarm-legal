Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Overview

VideoFarm ("the app") is a personal, self-hosted tool run by a single operator on their own hardware. The app does not operate a server, collect user data from third parties, or share data with any external entity beyond the social media platforms the operator has explicitly connected.

2. What data the app handles

To function, the app stores the following data locally on the operator's own machine:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens for connected accounts (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok).
  • Generated video files, scripts, thumbnails, and metadata.
  • Application logs and pipeline state.

None of this data is transmitted to the developer or to any third party other than the platforms the operator has connected (and only for the purpose of publishing content the operator explicitly chose to publish).

3. What data the app does not collect

  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
  • No cookies.
  • No personally identifiable information about visitors to this website.

4. Third-party services

When the operator connects an account, the app communicates directly with the respective platform's API (YouTube Data API, Instagram Graph API or instagrapi, TikTok Open API). Those platforms' own privacy policies apply to data handled on their side:

  • YouTube / Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta / Instagram: https://privacycenter.instagram.com/policy
  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data retention

Locally stored tokens and content remain on the operator's machine until manually deleted. The operator can revoke any connected account's access at any time through that platform's own security settings.

6. Children's privacy

The app is not intended for use by children under 13.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
